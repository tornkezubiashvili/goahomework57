Mercedes’ stylish coupe-style mid-size sedan, the CLS, survived for two generations only and was never replaced after MY2018 as sedans fell out of favor with buyers,
who are increasingly buying crossovers and SUVs. For the 2007-2018 model years, the Mercedes-AMG CLS 63 carried the flag as the quickest and most powerful CLS you could buy,
dispensing massive performance via thundering V8 engines, first a naturally aspirated 6.2L and later a twin-turbocharged 5.5L V8.
The last one was sold in 2017 and there will never be another, though AMG’s V8 powertrain lives on in other Mercedes models.
